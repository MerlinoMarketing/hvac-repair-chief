# HVAC Maintenance in Boca Raton, FL

Your air conditioning system works harder in South Florida than almost anywhere else in the country. Running nearly twelve months a year against intense heat and humidity takes a toll on every component. Routine preventive maintenance is the single most effective thing you can do to avoid unexpected breakdowns, reduce energy costs, and maximize the lifespan of your HVAC investment.

HVAC Repair Chief offers comprehensive maintenance plans and one-time tune-up services throughout Boca Raton and Palm Beach County.

## What South Florida HVAC Maintenance Includes

A professional maintenance visit from HVAC Repair Chief covers your entire system:

**Outdoor Unit (Condenser)**
- Coil inspection and cleaning
- Refrigerant pressure check
- Capacitor and contactor testing
- Fan motor inspection and lubrication
- Electrical connection tightening
- Clearance check around the unit

**Indoor Unit (Air Handler)**
- Evaporator coil inspection and cleaning
- Blower wheel inspection
- Filter assessment and replacement recommendation
- Condensate drain flush and treatment
- Electrical component check
- Ductwork connection inspection

**System Performance Verification**
- Thermostat calibration and testing
- Temperature differential measurement
- Airflow assessment
- Operating efficiency evaluation
