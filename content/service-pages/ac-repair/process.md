# Our AC Repair Process in Boca Raton

HVAC Repair Chief follows a consistent, transparent process for every AC repair call in Boca Raton and South Florida. Here is what you can expect from start to finish.

## Step 1: Schedule Your Service Call

Call us or book online. We offer flexible scheduling including same-day and emergency appointments throughout Boca Raton, Delray Beach, Boynton Beach, and surrounding areas. We give you a service window and send a confirmation.

## Step 2: Technician Arrives On Time

Our licensed technician arrives in a clearly marked HVAC Repair Chief vehicle, stocked with tools and common replacement parts. We respect your home — we wear shoe covers and clean up our work area before leaving.

## Step 3: Complete System Diagnostic

Before we touch anything, we diagnose the full system. This includes checking refrigerant pressure, electrical components, thermostat calibration, airflow, coil condition, and drainage. We do not guess — we find the actual root cause.

## Step 4: Upfront Repair Quote

After the diagnostic, we present a clear, itemized quote before any work begins. No surprise charges. You approve the price before we proceed. In Boca Raton, we price competitively and do not add hidden fees.

## Step 5: Repair Completed

With your approval, we complete the repair. Most jobs are finished in the same visit. We carry capacitors, contactors, fan motors, refrigerant, and other high-demand parts on the truck so we can get your system running without a return trip.

## Step 6: System Test and Verification

After the repair, we run the system through a full operational test. We measure supply air temperature, verify refrigerant levels, check electrical readings, and confirm the thermostat is responding correctly. We do not leave until the system is cooling properly.

## Step 7: Maintenance Recommendations

Before we go, we share honest maintenance tips specific to your system and your home. South Florida conditions are hard on HVAC equipment. We help you understand what to watch for and when to schedule your next preventive tune-up.

## Step 8: Workmanship Guarantee

Every repair is backed by our guarantee. If the same issue recurs within the warranty period, we return at no additional labor charge. That is our commitment to Boca Raton homeowners.
