# Duct Cleaning FAQs -- Boca Raton, FL

## How often should air ducts be cleaned?

Most industry experts recommend duct cleaning every three to five years for typical homes. In South Florida, the high humidity and year-round AC operation may warrant more frequent cleaning. Homes with pets, smokers, allergy sufferers, or recent renovations should consider cleaning more often.

## Can duct cleaning help with mold in my home?

If mold is present inside your ductwork, cleaning is an important step -- but it must be paired with identifying and correcting the moisture source. Cleaning ducts without fixing the underlying humidity issue will allow mold to return. HVAC Repair Chief identifies contributing factors and recommends appropriate solutions.

## Does duct cleaning really make a difference?

Yes, particularly in South Florida where high humidity and year-round operation create conditions that accumulate contaminants more quickly. Homes with visible mold, musty odors, or significant dust buildup at vents consistently show measurable improvements in air quality after cleaning.

## How long does duct cleaning take?

A typical single-family home in Boca Raton takes three to five hours depending on duct system size and complexity. Larger homes or systems with significant contamination may take longer.

## Will duct cleaning make a mess in my home?

No. We use sealed, negative-pressure equipment that captures debris as it is removed. We protect your furniture and floors during the process and clean up completely before leaving.

## Should I have my ducts cleaned before or after HVAC replacement?

We recommend cleaning before installing a new system. Starting a new system in clean ductwork ensures you are not immediately contaminating new components with old debris.

## Do you clean dryer vents too?

Yes. Dryer vent cleaning is an important safety service that prevents fire hazards from lint buildup. Ask about adding dryer vent cleaning when you schedule duct cleaning.
