# Our AC Installation Process in Boca Raton

HVAC Repair Chief follows a rigorous installation process designed to deliver maximum efficiency, comfort, and longevity for every system we install in Boca Raton and South Florida.

## Step 1: Free In-Home Estimate

We start with a no-obligation, in-home evaluation. Our comfort specialist visits your home, inspects the existing system and ductwork, and takes measurements. We do not provide quotes over the phone without seeing your home first -- proper sizing requires it.

## Step 2: Manual J Load Calculation

We calculate the exact cooling capacity your home needs using industry-standard Manual J methodology. This accounts for your home dimensions, insulation, window types, ceiling height, and Boca Raton climate data. Proper sizing is the foundation of a successful installation.

## Step 3: System Selection and Proposal

Based on the load calculation, your budget, and your efficiency goals, we present equipment options with clear pricing. We explain the tradeoffs between different efficiency tiers and help you choose confidently. No pressure, no upselling.

## Step 4: Permit Application

For most installations in Boca Raton and Palm Beach County, we apply for the required mechanical permit. We handle the paperwork so you do not have to.

## Step 5: Installation Day

Our installation crew arrives on time, lays down protective coverings, and begins work. The process includes:
- Safe removal and disposal of the old system
- Installation of the new air handler and outdoor condenser unit
- Refrigerant line connections, insulation, and pressure testing
- Electrical connections and dedicated circuit verification
- New or reconnected condensate drain routing
- Thermostat installation and programming

## Step 6: System Commissioning

After installation, we charge the system with the correct refrigerant quantity, verify electrical readings, and run a full performance test. We measure supply and return air temperatures to confirm the system is achieving proper temperature differential and operating efficiently.

## Step 7: Homeowner Walkthrough

Before we leave, we walk you through your new system. We show you how to operate the thermostat, explain filter change intervals, and share maintenance tips specific to South Florida's climate.

## Step 8: Permit Inspection

We coordinate the county or city inspection required to close the permit. Once approved, your installation is fully code-compliant and documented.
