# Our Heating Repair Process in Boca Raton

HVAC Repair Chief takes a systematic approach to every heating repair in Boca Raton. We do not guess -- we diagnose.

## Step 1: Schedule Your Heating Service Call

Call us or book online. We offer flexible scheduling and priority service during cold fronts. We serve all of Boca Raton and Palm Beach County.

## Step 2: Technician Arrives Ready to Work

Our technician arrives with a fully stocked service vehicle. We carry common heat pump components including reversing valves, capacitors, contactors, defrost boards, and refrigerant. Most repairs are completed in the same visit.

## Step 3: Full Heating System Diagnostic

We run a complete diagnostic before recommending any repair. For heat pump systems, this includes:
- Checking refrigerant pressure in heating mode
- Testing the reversing valve operation
- Inspecting the defrost control board and sensors
- Testing capacitors and contactors
- Checking electric strip heat elements and fuses
- Verifying thermostat operation and heat staging

## Step 4: Clear, Upfront Repair Quote

After the diagnostic, we explain what we found and present a clear quote. You approve the price before we begin any work.

## Step 5: Repair Performed

We complete the approved repair using quality parts. Our goal is a permanent fix, not a temporary patch.

## Step 6: Operational Test in Heating Mode

We run the system through a full heating cycle and verify performance. We confirm the system is reaching target temperatures and operating at correct refrigerant pressures.

## Step 7: Recommendations and Next Steps

We share any additional observations about your system and recommend preventive steps to avoid future issues. If we see signs of other developing problems, we tell you honestly so you can plan ahead.
